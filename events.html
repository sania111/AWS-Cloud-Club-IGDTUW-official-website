AWS COMMUNITY DAY

The success of 'The Student Community Day' organized by the AWS Cloud Club Society was beyond measure! With an impressive lineup of speakers like Jen Looper, Aditi Sawhney, Varsha Verma, Kristine Howard, Gargee Bhatnagar, Omshree Bhutani, Payal Gupta, and Rajani, covering diverse topics ranging from career pathways to advanced AWS Cloud practices, the event garnered immense interest and engagement from attendees. Participants left feeling inspired, equipped with valuable knowledge, and motivated to pursue their goals in cloud computing and beyond. The positive feedback received from attendees, coupled with the insightful discussions and networking opportunities, underscored the event's resounding success in empowering students and fostering a vibrant community of learning and growth.
